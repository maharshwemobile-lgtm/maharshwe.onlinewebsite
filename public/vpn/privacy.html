Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to MaharShwe VPN, operated by Mahar Shwe Mobile. It explains what information is used when you install, open, connect, scan keys, receive notifications, use free servers, or contact support.

Information the app uses

  • VPN access keys and server names are stored on your device so the app can connect to the servers you add.
  • Free server keys are downloaded from MaharShwe's Google Sheet so the app can show the current free servers.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token may be sent to the MaharShwe server so update notifications can be delivered.
  • Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers, device information, and ad interaction data when ads are shown to free server users.
  • If you contact support by Telegram, phone, or payment channels, the information you send is handled by that support channel.

Camera and QR scanning

The camera permission is used only to scan Outline/VPN access key QR codes. QR images are processed on the device and are not uploaded by the scanner.

Notifications

The app may ask for notification permission. Notifications are used for app updates, service notices, and support messages. You can disable notifications in Android settings.

Payments

Payments for paid VPN keys are handled outside the app through the payment details provided by Mahar Shwe Mobile. Do not send sensitive banking passwords or one-time codes through support chat.

Data sharing

We use service providers such as Firebase Cloud Messaging and Google AdMob to operate notifications and ads. Their processing is governed by their own terms and privacy practices.
